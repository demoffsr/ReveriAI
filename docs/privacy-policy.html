Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 3, 2026

Reveri ("we", "our", "the app") is a dream journal application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data Storage

All dream entries, audio recordings, and personal preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not have access to your dream content.

2. AI Features

When you use AI-powered features (dream title generation, interpretation, dream images), the text of your dream is sent to our server for processing. This data is:

  • Used solely to generate the AI response
  • Not stored on our servers after processing
  • Not used to train AI models
  • Not shared with third parties

3. Speech Recognition

Voice recording and speech-to-text processing happen on your device using Apple's Speech framework. Audio data is not sent to our servers.

4. Analytics

We collect anonymous usage analytics (feature usage counts, session duration) to improve the app. This data contains no personally identifiable information and cannot be linked to individual users.

5. Data We Do Not Collect

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Photos or files outside the app
  • Browsing history
  • Data from other apps on your device

6. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — backend infrastructure for AI feature processing and anonymous analytics
  • OpenAI — AI model provider for dream title and interpretation generation

These services process data according to their respective privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

Your dream data remains on your device until you delete it. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. AI processing data is not retained on our servers.

8. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
